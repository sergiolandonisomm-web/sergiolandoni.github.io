My work connects vineyards, producers, gastronomy and international audiences through real presence in the territory and meaningful storytelling.

This grounded approach helps position Neuquén and Patagonia as distinctive cool-climate wine regions on the global stage.

Patagonia Argentina · National and International Projection

About Sergio Landoni

I am Sergio Landoni, a territory-based sommelier working from Patagonia, Argentina.

I connect the authentic origins of Neuquén and Patagonian wines with audiences and professionals seeking genuine terroir-driven experiences.

My work is rooted in direct presence in the vineyards and wineries, allowing me to interpret wine beyond technical tasting — through its landscape, climate and cultural context.

What I offer

  • Territorial Identity — Deep storytelling and strategic positioning.
  • Connection — Bridging wine, gastronomy, tourism and culture.
  • International Projection — Linking Patagonia with global wine networks.

I help strengthen the international recognition of Patagonia’s cool-climate wines, emphasizing freshness, elegance and unique typicity.
